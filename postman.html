Team plan – where it wins

  • ✅ Lowest upfront cost: ~$3.2k/year vs $8.2k (saves ~$5k annually)
  • ✅ Perfect for single team with no strict compliance
  • ✅ Unlimited API builder & collaboration basics
  • ✅ Enough for many startups and small teams
  • ⚠️ Missing SSO (add-on cost), audit logs, and internal catalog

  • ✅ SSO + SCIM → automated onboarding/offboarding, huge security gain

  • ✅ Partner workspaces → collaborate with agencies without extra seats
  • ✅ 1M API calls/month → heavy automation for QA
  • ✅ Secret scanner prevents credential leaks

Final verdict: Team plan is the smart choice for your budget

With 10 engineers, 200 APIs, and a mix of backend, mobile, and QA, the Team plan offers everything you need to collaborate effectively at ~$3,200/year – saving over $5,000 compared to Enterprise. While Enterprise adds advanced security and governance, those features may not be mandatory for your current scale.

  • ✔️ Team plan wins on cost – ideal for startups and mid-sized teams without strict compliance needs
  • ✔️ Includes shared workspaces, unlimited APIs, and basic RBAC – enough for most daily workflows
  • ✔️ Optional SSO add-on available if you later need single sign-on
  • ⚠️ Enterprise is only necessary if you require audit logs, private API network, or automated provisioning (SCIM)

Recommendation: Start with the Team plan. You can always upgrade to Enterprise later if security or governance demands grow. For now, the cost savings are significant and the feature set is more than adequate for your team of 14.
